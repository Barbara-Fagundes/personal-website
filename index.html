About

Helping people use new tools without losing sight of how people learn.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering education, instructional design, educational research, and technology adoption. Across research, teaching, and training, I focus on how learners build clarity in uncertain situations, especially when working in teams or learning new digital tools.

I have worked across higher education, K-12, and professional learning contexts, including first-year engineering, K-2 computational thinking, online course design, and generative AI adoption in computing education. I care about making complex work feel structured, practical, and genuinely supportive.
